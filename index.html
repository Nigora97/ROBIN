More About Me

Hi! I’m Robin Williams, a passionate designer and creative thinker. Over the past 10 years, I’ve worked with some of the biggest names in the tech and design industries, creating impactful experiences and meaningful connections through my work.

Biography

Born and raised in Italy, I always found inspiration in art, nature, and culture. I studied design at the University of Milan and started my career with Dribbble. My journey led me to work with companies like Facebook and Google, where I specialized in product design and interaction design.

Skills

  • Product Design
  • Interaction Design
  • Graphic Design
  • Prototyping (Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch)
  • UX Research and Strategy
  • Creative Thinking

Key Projects

  1. Google Play Redesign: Led the interaction design team to create a more intuitive and user-friendly interface.
  2. Facebook Internal Tools: Designed and implemented tools that increased employee productivity by 20%.
  3. Dribbble Illustrations: Created hundreds of unique and engaging illustrations for the platform.
